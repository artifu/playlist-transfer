About

A playlist transfer tool built around trust.

PlaylistXfer started as a technical spike for one real problem: moving Spotify playlists into Apple Music without paying for a black-box transfer.

PlaylistXfer is intentionally review-first. The product shows what it found, what it matched, and what it could not safely move before asking for Apple Music permission.

What makes it different

The core idea is not just transfer speed. It is transfer confidence. Public playlist preview, match groups, candidate review, and a final receipt all exist so the user can see what happened.

Current status

The web MVP is live, and the iOS app is in active development. The product currently focuses on Spotify to Apple Music and public Spotify playlist links.

Open project

The project is documented publicly for people who want to understand or replicate the transfer flow. You can follow development on the PlaylistXfer GitHub repository.
