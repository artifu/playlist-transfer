Why these guides exist

A playlist is not a folder of identical files shared by every streaming service. Spotify and Apple Music each maintain their own catalog, territory availability, identifiers, editions, and metadata. A title that looks identical can refer to a studio recording, live performance, remaster, radio edit, clean version, or regional release.

PlaylistXfer is designed around that uncertainty. It previews the source, separates confident matches from ambiguous ones, and keeps questionable tracks out of the destination until a person reviews them. These guides explain those decisions in plain language so you can finish a transfer without treating the result like a black box.
